# Discover & Join

**Discover & Join** is where you find ecosystems to participate in. Open it from **Discover & Join** in the navigation. Browsing is read-only and needs no wallet; joining does.

## Browse

Search ecosystems by DID, and expand any card to see its credential schemas (with issuer/verifier validity periods and permission modes), its trust deposit, and its governance framework. Each card offers **EGF** (open the governance framework) and **View Ecosystem** (open the full detail page).

![Discover and join ecosystems](/img/frontend/discover.png)

## Join an ecosystem

Open an ecosystem and click **Join** to start the [Onboarding Process](./onboarding-process): a guided wizard where you pick a credential schema and role, accept the governance framework, choose a validator, and submit your permission request.

OPEN schemas let you self-create the permission immediately; schemas that require validation route your request to a grantor or the ecosystem owner, and it then appears in your [Pending Tasks](./pending-tasks). For the permission mechanics, see [Permissions](./ecosystems/permissions/intro).
