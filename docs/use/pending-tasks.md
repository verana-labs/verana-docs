# Pending Tasks

The **Pending Tasks** page collects every action waiting on you across all your ecosystems, plus actions waiting on your grantees. Open it from **Pending Tasks** in the navigation; the nav badge shows how many are outstanding.

Tasks are grouped as a tree, ecosystem → credential schema → permission, so you can see where each pending action sits. Typical items include:

- validation requests to approve or reject for permissions you grant,
- renewals and deposits coming due,
- pending actions on permissions held by your grantees.

Each entry links into the relevant permission so you can act on it (approve a validation, renew, cancel a request). See [Permissions](./ecosystems/permissions/intro) for the individual flows.
